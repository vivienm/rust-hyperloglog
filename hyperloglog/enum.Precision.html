Expand description

The precision of the hyperloglog, that is, the number of bytes used for sharding accross registers.

A hyperloglog with precision p will have 2^p registers and require a proportional amount of memory.

Default precision is 12 bits, i.e. 4096 registers.

Examples

use hyperloglog::{HyperLogLog, Precision};

let hll = HyperLogLog::<i32>::with_precision(Precision::P12);
